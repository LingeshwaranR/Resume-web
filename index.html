Intellikart

For Queue Less And Effortless Billing

Shopping at big malls has become a daily activity in metro cities. There is a huge rush in the mall, special occasions and weekends. This rush in the market is mainly a problem when we are standing in the queue for billing the purchases. People picks different items and drop in the trolley. After that only there exists the toughest part of the shopping, the billing and its queue. Our aim is to develop a solution for the above mentioned challenge using IOT and mobile application. To overcome the problems stated above and to improve the existing system, we have designed a trolley which is going to be really SMART. This can be done by simple reading the barcode from the products and a barcode reader with a circuit is attached on the shopping trolley.

Description

  • Automatic billing of products by using barcode reader will be a more essential option in the future of shopping
  • The system based on barcode technique is efficient, speed and shows amazing performance.
  • This system using IOT will save time of customers and manpower required in stores and cost associated with the product.

Farm2Home

A Tool For Farmer Welfare

Farmer ->consumer = Farm2Home
We have a plan of implementing a mobile app named FARM2HOMEthat helps to overcome the farmer’s issues. The app consists of two ends, one is for farmers and the other is for buyers(people). The farmers can login to the app and can start posting their products with its dynamic rates (rate is fixed by the local agriculture committee) after setting up their personal details like name, location, contact etc. At the other end the buyers can login to the app and can buy the vegetables by seeing the nearby farmers posts (posts are filtered with location of the buyers) and can add them to cart and after all the commodities are purchased they are shipped to their residence. Our FARM2HOMEfocuses on both the farmer and the customer end by neglecting the middle man and enriching farmer’s welfare and reducing the workload of buyers and giving them pure green vegetables at their doorstep

Description

  • Generation of production graph which in order to prevent the profitless income for farmers
  • A Platform to sell the farmers product directly to the customers without involvement of middle man need to be introduced.

Key Feature

Production Graph:
The vegetables ,fruits which will be in demand is monitored using data analytics and graph is been generated. And with help of these graphs production techniques is been generated.
Production Techniques:
Based on demand production graph is generated each farmer will be suggested with individual instruction to do farming for their welfare
